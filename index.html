Will Monahan Filmmaker, Designer

Will Monahan is an independent filmmaker, photographer, designer, and musician from Toronto, Canada, currently living in New York City. Will discovered and began to foster his passion for filmmaking growing up in Toronto. After attending the University of Toronto Schools for six years, Will is currently attending the Tisch School of the Arts at New York University, where he is studying Film & Television Production.

Will began making short films with his family and friends when he was 12 years old. As he grew older, he began to refine his filmmaking technique, eventually deciding to pursue an education, and ultimately a career, in film and television. His experiences working in photography, design, and music have all contributed to Will's filmmaking.

Will's body of work includes experience in animation, documentary, narrative, and commercial film, as well as work in photography, music, and other types of art and design. Will has also worked in exhibition through both TIFF and BAFTA New York.
